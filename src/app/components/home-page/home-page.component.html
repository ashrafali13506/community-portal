Featured Posts

Dr. Anthony

MBBS, MS (Ortho)

Please Note: This is an educational piece that is being provided to all PLM members regardless of condition(s). Join us for a conversation with Dr. Mary C. (Kate) Burke, MD, Senior Medical Advisor at PatientsLikeMe, about living with MDD. Throughout the conversation, Dr. Kate will: Share information about MDD symptoms Outline what to expect from the evaluation Detail common treatment options Explain goal attainment strategy and how it can be used to help manage MDD Additionally, PLM members with MDD will share their experience with MDD and some tips and strategies they have used to help regulate their condition. Be sure to join the conversation – you can submit questions in the comments that could be featured in a future Q&A follow-up.

Dr. Jane

MBBS, MS

The Impact of Individualized Medicine: Q&ASpecialuests Dr. Jane Topol and patient advocate Christine Von Raesfeld discuss how DNA health data can affect your health journey, plus bonus Q&A. Recently, PatientsLikeMe’s Associate Director of Community Management, Brad Hornback, sat down with Dr. Eric Topol (Founder & Director at Scripps Research Translational Institute) and Christine Von Raesefeld (Patient Advocate) to discuss the topic of genomics and medicine, including how genomic data can affect the healthcare journey and how individuals may receive their own DNA results by participating in the All of Us Research Program.
